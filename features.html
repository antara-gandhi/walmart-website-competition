Features

A continuously updating list of everything Wheely and Walmart can do for you.


Health

Walmart is dedicated to the health and safety of our customers. That is why Wheely includes a biometric handle.

With this feature, and the customer's consent, Wheely will track your:

  • Heart rate
  • Temperature
  • Speed

Doing so will allow us to compare it for sudden spikes or drops and, in that case, alert our associates for possible emergencies.

Wheely also includes a general "Call for Help" button, in case you need an associates assistance ASAP.

Family

Walmart understands the need to shop quickly and effeciently for all your family needs. That is why you can scan as you shop, pay at the cart, and opt for a self-driving cart.

We also understand that we have to move slower for our little ones. That is why our self-driving option also includes a slower auto-speed for a more relaxed and safe shopping experience.

Check out more about these features here.

Convenience

We want to make your shopping experience as comfortable and convenient as possible. Wheely's cart includes bags on the side built in for your personal bagging needs. The bags can come out from a simple pull, making it easy and accessible for all.

Additionally, you can now take Wheely out to your car when you're all set. Once you've unloaded, press "All Set" and Wheely will return to the nearest parking lot cart holder!
