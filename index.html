Meet Wheely

Your personal Walmart smart cart assistant.


Over the past year and a half, Walmart has been commited to the health of our community. With COVID-19 testing and increasingly innovative ways to shop contactlessly, Walmart is working to understand how to bridge technology and Walmart's customers' needs. With Wheely, Walmart brings a convinient, family-friendly and COVID-19 concious solution to the table. Get shopping done faster, easier, and safer with Wheely.

Wheely's Main Features

Scan as you go

Take away the hassle of checkout! With Wheely's inbuilt scanning system, you can scan your items as you shop. See your discounts and savings in real time!

Self-driving option

For all our busy folks out there, Wheely has the option to drive automatically in a few simple steps:

  1. Download the Walmart App
  2. Create your personal grocery list
  3. Upload to your Wheely shopping cart in-store
  4. Have Wheely create a personal shopping route for you and let him take the wheel!

Pay when ready

In addition to scanning on the go, skip the hassle of lines with contactless payments. Whenever you're ready, just click pay and insert your credit card. It's that simple!
